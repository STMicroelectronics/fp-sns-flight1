Purpose

Supported Devices and Boards

  • STM32F401RE devices and STM32F401RE-Nucleo board RevB
  • STM32L476RG devices and STM32L476RG-Nucleo board RevB
  • X-NUCLEO-IDB05A2 Bluetooth Low energy expansion boards based on the BlueNRG-M0 module for STM32 Nucleo
  • X-NUCLEO-53L3A2 Multi-targets ranging sensor expansion board based on VL53L3CX for STM32 Nucleo.
  • X-NUCLEO-NFC04A1 NFC/RFID tag IC expansion board based on ST25DV04K (optional)
